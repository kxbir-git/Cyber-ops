About Cyber-Ops

A Community of Future Defenders

We are a student-driven initiative fostering hands-on cybersecurity skills through workshops, competitions, and real-world challenges. Guided by expert faculty, we build the next generation of ethical hackers and digital defenders.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about joining and participating in Cyber-Ops.

Who can join Cyber-Ops?
+
Any student enrolled in the CSE Department at Integral University can join, regardless of their cybersecurity experience level. We welcome beginners and advanced learners alike.
Do I need prior experience?
+
Not at all! We have beginner-friendly resources and dedicated onboarding sessions for newcomers. All you need is curiosity and a willingness to learn.
What tools will I learn?
+
You'll work with industry-standard tools like Kali Linux, Burp Suite, Wireshark, Metasploit, Nmap, and more — the same tools used by professional security researchers.
How often do events happen?
+
We host weekly learning sessions, monthly challenges, and participate in external competitions throughout the academic year. There's always something to engage with.
Will I get a certificate?
+
Yes! Active members receive participation certificates, and top performers earn recognition letters that can be added to their resumes and LinkedIn profiles.
